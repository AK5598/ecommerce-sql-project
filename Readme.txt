# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Logistics Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📝 Project Description

This SQL project simulates an end-to-end e-commerce business scenario.  
The database was created in MySQL using Excel files for Customers, Products, Orders, Shipments, and Trucks.  
The goal was to answer real-world business questions related to customer spending, sales trends, logistics, and operational efficiency.

---

## 🗃️ Tables Used

- `customers` – Customer profiles and regions
- `orders` – Customer orders and dates
- `order_items` – Products ordered and quantities
- `products` – Product catalog with prices and categories
- `shipments` – Shipping details and delivery status
- `trucks` – Truck capacity and IDs

---

## ❓ Business Questions Answered

1. Total number of orders placed  
2. Top 5 customers by total spending  
3. Best-selling products  
4. Revenue by product category  
5. Monthly order trends  
6. Truck utilization (shipments per truck)  
7. Region-wise revenue analysis  
8. Average order value (AOV)  
9. Customers with repeat orders  
10. Shipment status breakdown (Delivered, Pending, etc.)
